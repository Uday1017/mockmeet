# 📧 What Users Will Experience

## When You Schedule a Mock Interview

### Step 1: Propose a Session
**Interviewer** proposes a session:
- Date & Time: Tomorrow, 6:00 PM
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Target Role: SDE-1
- Difficulty: Medium

### Step 2: Confirm the Session
**Both users** need to confirm:
- Interviewee confirms ✅
- Interviewer confirms ✅

### Step 3: Automatic Magic Happens! ✨

Once both confirm, you'll receive:

#### 📧 Email Notification
```
Subject: Invitation: MockMeet Interview: SDE-1 @ Tomorrow 6:00 PM

You have been invited to the following event:

MockMeet Interview: SDE-1
When: Tomorrow, 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM
Where: Google Meet (link below)

Mock Interview Session

Interviewer: John Doe
Interviewee: Jane Smith

Difficulty: medium
Target Role: SDE-1

Prepare well and good luck! 🚀

Powered by MockMeet

Join with Google Meet: https://meet.google.com/abc-defg-hij

View event details: [Link to Google Calendar]
```

#### 📅 Calendar Event
The event automatically appears in your Google Calendar with:
- Title: "MockMeet Interview: SDE-1"
- Time: Tomorrow, 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM
- Location: Google Meet link
- Reminders:
  - 📧 Email 24 hours before
  - 📧 Email 1 hour before
  - 🔔 Popup 10 minutes before

#### 🔗 Meet Link in App
In the MockMeet Sessions page, you'll see:
- Session details
- **"Join Meeting"** button
- Click to open Google Meet

## Pro Tips

### For Best Experience:
1. **Use your primary email** when registering
2. **Check spam folder** for first invite
3. **Accept calendar invite** to confirm attendance
4. **Join 5 minutes early** to test audio/video
5. **Keep notifications on** for reminders

### Troubleshooting:
- **Didn't receive email?** Check spam folder
- **Can't join meeting?** Copy link from Sessions page
- **Wrong time?** Check your timezone settings
- **Need to reschedule?** Cancel and create new session

### What We Access:
- ✅ Your email (to send invites)

## Questions?

**How do I join the meeting?**
- Click "Join Meeting" in the Sessions page
- Or click the Meet link in your email
- Or open the calendar event and click "Join with Google Meet"

**What if I miss the session?**
- Your reputation score will be affected
- Credits are still transferred
- You can reschedule with the same person

**Can I reschedule?**
- Cancel the current session
- Propose a new session with new time

**What if the other person doesn't show up?**
- Wait 15 minutes
- System will mark them as no-show
- Your credits will be refunded
